# AI Challenge

A self-directed curriculum for strengthening the mathematical and engineering foundations behind modern AI systems, with an emphasis on medical AI, industrial vision, and reliable model deployment.

Inspired by [Scott Young's MIT Challenge](https://www.scotthyoung.com/blog/myprojects/mit-challenge-2/), this page tracks courses, outputs, and implementation notes that can later become reproducible blog posts or public project artifacts.

| Field | Plan |
| --- | --- |
| Focus | Deep learning theory, computer vision, and applied model engineering |
| Output | Lecture notes, solved assignments, implementation logs, and blog writeups |
| Completion rule | A course is complete only when the notes and at least one reproducible artifact are publishable |
| Link policy | Placeholder links are avoided until the artifact is ready to share |
